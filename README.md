# sudoku
A sudoku game board with over 1 million different games

## Inspiration 

I LOVE sudoku. Time passes by when I play it on my phone and that's not always a good thing obviously. So, after taking inspiration from people 3d printing their favorite board games online, I decided to transform sudoku into one. Thus, I can play sudoku off my phone as well and have learned more about electronics in the process. It would also be cool to show some friends!

## How it works 

After browsing on Kaggle for a little, I found a dataset of over 1 million sudoky games along with their solutions.
The idea is simple: the device picks a random sudoku game and displays the quiz. The player uses the custom macropad to move around and place the numbers. If he's wrong, it will display an error and otherwise it is correctly placed. I am thinking of even making a simple app so the user can keep track of all the games he played and how much time he took to complete each one. 

## Dataset

The following links to the dataset used for this project: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bryanpark/sudoku

## Components 

- ESP32
- 1 Digit Displays
- Display Screen
- Rechargeable Battery
